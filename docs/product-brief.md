# SermonLife Product Brief

## Vision

SermonLife helps believers move from hearing a sermon to living it through organized notes, reflection, prayer, and weekly action.

## Target User

- Individual church members who want to remember and apply sermons
- Small group leaders who track discussion and prayer points
- Pastors or ministry teams who want simple follow-up engagement

## Core User Problems

1. Sermon notes are scattered across notebooks, screenshots, and apps.
2. Key takeaways are forgotten before they become action.
3. Prayer points and weekly spiritual habits are not connected to sermon content.

## MVP

### Primary Features

- Save sermons with title, speaker, date, scripture, and series
- Capture sermon notes and key takeaways
- Convert takeaways into action steps and prayer points
- View a weekly dashboard with habits and active reflection items

### Nice-to-Have After MVP

- Audio or video sermon linking
- Church-wide shared sermon feed
- AI summaries and reflection prompts
- Group discussion templates

## Suggested Information Architecture

- Home
- Sermons
- Notes
- Prayer
- Profile

## Product Tone

- Calm
- Reflective
- Warm
- Trustworthy
